AdvanceTech Community Centre is a valuable resource for anyone looking to improve their digital skills. They offer a wide range of classes and workshops, catering to individuals at all levels of expertise. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced user, you'll find a program that suits your needs.

From basic computer skills to advanced coding and digital design, AdvanceTech's curriculum covers a variety of topics. Their experienced instructors provide a supportive and engaging learning environment, helping students to develop their confidence and abilities.

Beyond their educational offerings, AdvanceTech is also committed to fostering community development. They collaborate with local businesses and organizations to promote digital inclusion and economic growth. By providing access to technology and training, AdvanceTech is empowering individuals and strengthening their communities.

OUR PROGRAMS CONSIST OF THE FOLLOWING:

TRAINING:

  • COMPUTER BASICS: How to use a computer, mouse, and keyboard.
  • INTERNET SAFETY: Protecting yourself online and avoiding scams.
  • EMAIL: Sending, receiving, and organizing emails.
  • MICROSOFT OFFICE OR GOOGLE SUITE: Using programs like Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.
  • SOCIAL MEDIA: Understanding and using social media platforms responsibly.
  • CODING AND WEB DESIGN: Creating basic websites.
  • PHOTOGRAPHY: Taking and editing photos.
  • JOB SEARCHING: Finding and applying for jobs online.

PROGRAM AREAS:

  • DIGITAL ART: Creating art using computers.
  • COMPUTER BASICS: Understanding how computers work and using them effectively.
  • CODING: Learning to write computer programs.
