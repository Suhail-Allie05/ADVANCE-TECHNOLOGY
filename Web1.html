In a world where technology is advancing at an unprecedented rate, many individuals are being left behind. Welcome to AdvanceTech Community Centre, where we're bridging the digital divide. AdvanceTech Community Centre is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing digital literacy programs to the communities. Our centre offers a safe and supportive environment for individuals to learn, grow, and thrive in the digital age.

Mission and Vision

Mission:

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities through digital literacy, fostering a culture of innovation and social connection. Our vision is a digitally inclusive society where everyone has access to the opportunities and resources they need to succeed.

Vision:

Our vision is to cultivate a technologically proficient community that embraces innovation and creativity, enabling individuals to thrive in a rapidly changing digital landscape. We envision a future where everyone has access to the tools and knowledge necessary to succeed in the digital world.

The Digital Divide

This term refers to the disparity between individuals, communities, or societies that have access to modern information and communication technologies and those that do not. This gap can result from various factors, including socioeconomic status, geographic location, education, and age.

Internet:

Access to the internet is essential for communication, information retrieval, and online services. A lack of internet access limits individuals' ability to engage in educational and professional opportunities.

Computers:

Computers are crucial tools for work, education, and personal use. Without access to computers, individuals may struggle to acquire the necessary skills for the digital age, limiting their career prospects and educational opportunities.

Smartphones:

Smartphones provide mobile access to the internet and a range of applications. They are increasingly used for communication, banking, education, and entertainment. Those without smartphones may miss out on important information and services.

Digital Literacy:

Digital literacy refers to the ability to effectively use digital technologies to find, evaluate, create, and communicate information. Individuals lacking digital literacy skills may face challenges in navigating technology, limiting their ability to participate fully in the digital world.
